Concept


I chose to rebrand Earth's Own Oat Milk by creating a new mascot character, side characters, storyboards for a commercial, and a billboard advertisement. This project was created for an illustration and storyboarding course.

Otis


Otis is the new mascot of Earth's Own Oat Milk. Otis is the young and cheerful aspiring farmer who will take over the oat farm from Lotus when they grow up. Otis loves nature, plants, and keeping active.

Lotus

Lotus is the grounded, experienced mentor who is teaching Otis the ways of the land and how to care for the oat fields.

Jeremiah, Eleni, and Hana

Jeremiah, Eleni, and Hana are kids come out ot play soccer by the fields where Otis and Lotus work. They ask Otis to join their soccer session and enjoy a drink of oat milk after some good physical activity.

Advertisement


I created an advertisement that may be used for a billboard. I used colours from the original oat milk carton and incorporated the existing plant leaf logo in the illustration of Otis holding a leaf shovel up.



Storyboards


I created storyboards for a TV commercial for Earth's Own Oat Milk. The story is about Otis learning about growing oats when suddenly he is invited to play soccer with some local kids. They all enjoy kicking the ball around until the ball goes flying into the field again and hits Lotus. Lotus plays it cool and shows off some soccer tricks. After all the physical activity, they all come together to enjoy some refreshing oat milk.
